Shibusa

The Japanese term, Shibusa, refers to an enriched yet subdued appearance of intrinsically fine quality with economy of form, line, and effort, producing a timeless tranquility. While balancing complexity with simplicity, the Shibusa brand aims to provide carefully crafted products to a broad range of consumers with a highly competitive price point.

Methods

Italian chef, Frank Prisinzano, rose to fame during the pandemic by sharing daily instructional videos of his accessible, upscale “methods” or recipes. He lovingly references the ingredients as “elements,” and the steps as the “flow.” His aim is to help people from all walks of life learn to cook through their senses and throw measurements out of the window.

Harvest Market

Socially conscious grocery store, Harvest Market, uses their annual report to show the company’s yearly growth to stockholders. This report can also be useful to incentivize new investors.

Native Organic Selections

The premium brand, Native, offers a wide array of consumable products found in local US grocery markets. The company places strong emphasis on quality of ingredients while remaining attainable to a broad socioeconomic audience. The company requested a logo creation and an expansive, cohesive redesign of multiple product packagings.

Dawn Patrol Coffee and Bakery

Dawn Patrol Coffee and Bakery, located just off the beach in Wilmington, NC, targets surfers looking for a caffeine boost or a snack before early morning paddle-outs. The company was in need of a logo design, storefront sign, and package design.
